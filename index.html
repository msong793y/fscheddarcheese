Welcome to Meta Sloth

Its napping time and Kay the sloth just wants to go home and take a nap. On her way back from the forest she encounters her greatest fear, pack of rats! But fear not, her trust companion Laney the cat will defend her. Survive the waves of rats to win!

Kay the Sloth: Use the WASD keys to move your Sloth. Dodge the rats

Laney the Cat Use mouse click to target where your Cat runs to. Hold on on the mouse button for a more powerful and faster charge.
