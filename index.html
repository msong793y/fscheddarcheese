Welcome to Cheddar Cheese

Its napping time and Cheddar the Hamster just wants to go home and take a nap. On his way back from the forest he encounters his greatest fear, pack of rats! But fear not, his trust companion Laney the cat will defend him. Survive the waves of rats to win!

Cheddar the Hamster: Use the WASD keys to move the hamster. Dodge the rats

Laney the Cat: Use mouse click to target where the Cat runs to. Hold on on the mouse button for a more powerful and faster charge.
